          Nike Joyride

          Why Beads?

          They're soft, they're bouncy and they form the core of an innovative new cushioning system designed to help make all the ways you move feel easy—even running.

          Nike Joyride Run Flyknit

          Shop All

          A Softer Cushioning System

          We spent years obsessing over the beads—finding the right size, shape and feel to deliver a soft, comfortable ride. Here's how it works: Every time you take a step—or stride—10,000+ beads shape around your foot to provide personalised cushioning and support, before responsively bouncing back to propel you forward.

          Absorbs the Impact

          That soft ride doesn't just go easy on your feet—it helps your whole body to feel good. The Nike Joyride cushioning system offers 14% better impact absorption when compared to some of our most trusted running shoes (tested with Nike Air Zoom Pegasus 36 and Nike Epic React FK2).

          Which Joyride Is Right For You?

          Nike Joyride Flyknit

          If you’re looking for a totally unique, completely personalized cushioning feel, this is the shoe for you. With four pods of tiny, squishy beads placed strategically throughout the midsole, the Nike Joyride Flyknit offers incredible comfort from heel to toe on your shakeout runs.

          Nike Joyride Dual Run

          This shoe feels like a more traditional running shoe, with two pods of tiny foam beads in the heel and midfoot for personalised cushioning where you need it most. The one-two punch of the beads in the most common areas of greatest impact and a responsive foam midsole move you easily from one stride to the next.
